# Meet Luna, Our Golden Retriever Puppy

![Luna the golden retriever puppy](/images/luna-puppy.jpg)

Last week, our family grew by one furry member. Let me introduce you to **Luna**, an 8-week-old golden retriever with the most expressive eyes you've ever seen.

> "A dog is the only thing on earth that loves you more than he loves himself." – Josh Billings

## The First Day Home

Bringing Luna home was an adventure. She was curious about everything – the garden hose, the squeaky toys we bought, and especially the cat (who is *not* amused).

### Luna's Favorite Things So Far:

1. **Chewing on shoes** – My favorite sneakers are now her favorite toys
2. **Chasing butterflies** – She hasn't caught one yet, but not for lack of trying
3. **Sleeping in sunbeams** – She finds every patch of sunlight in the house

![Luna sleeping in sunlight](/images/luna-sleeping.jpg)

## Training Progress

We've started basic training, and she's surprisingly smart:

- ✓ Sits on command (when treats are involved)
- ✓ Knows her name
- ⚠️ Working on "stay" (she's too excited to stay put)
- ⚠️ Potty training is... ongoing

## What's Next

In the coming weeks, I'll document:
- Her first trip to the dog park
- Meeting other dogs in the neighborhood
- The inevitable destruction of a stuffed toy

Stay tuned for more puppy adventures! 🐾
