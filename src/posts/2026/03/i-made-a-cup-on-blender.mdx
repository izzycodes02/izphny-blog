I was writing in my diary just a couple of minutes ago. I keep a physical diary, I have been for the last nearly 10 years. Because why not~ <Emoji type="cool" />

My current diary is Diary #3! <Emoji type="smilewide" /> The actual diary notebook was from a book/stationery store in <MyLink type="location" src="https://www.google.com/search?q=m%C3%BCnchen&sxsrf=ANbL-n5E7Z3yuzRAEnyGvBu8DBHYqFLjqw%3A1774821862335&gs_ssp=eJzj4tLP1TcwyjCLNyswYPTiyD28Jy85IzUPAErkB2A">München, Bavaria, Germany</MyLink>.

One of my favourite things to do with my diary is design the individual pages. I used to hand draw doodles and colour them in, but once I got my hands on big-girl money, I started buying stickers and sheets to add to them. <Emoji type="princess" />

Now, I mainly focus on using stickers, printed photos <Emoji type="camera" />, postcards from travels, and random scraps of paper I find meaningful/fun to add to my diary.
