I spent yesterday coding this blog so I neglected using Blender a bit. I know, I just started and I didn't even stick to my curriculum! But I held myself accountable and used Blender today <Emoji type="angel" />

Today was officially day 4 of learning Blender and my tasks were to make a cup and a book! <Emoji type="smilewide" /><Emoji type="disappointed" />

I initially made the cup. I already learnt of Grab (G), Scale (S), Rotate (R), and Extrude (E in Edit Mode). But I didn't know how to add the hole into the cup to be... you know, a cup? So, I had to Google that and it was the Insert (I) key and moving it down! And it made the hole! <Emoji type="nerd" />

I played around more and added a handle but that was me purely messing around and figuring it out myself!
